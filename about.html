Our Journey

Rotaract Club of Coimbatore City was charted on 22 July 2021. Active in RY 22-23 and the first two months of RY 23-24, the club was officially terminated in RY 23-24 due to low membership and recharted in RY 24-25.

Until RY 25-26 the club was under RID 3201 before district separation into RID 3205 and RID 3206.
